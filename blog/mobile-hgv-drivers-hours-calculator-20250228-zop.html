Mobile HGV Drivers Hours Calculator

February 28, 2024 | 15 minute read

In today's digital age, managing HGV drivers' hours effectively is crucial for fleet operations. Mobile calculators have become essential tools for ensuring compliance and safety.

Understanding HGV Drivers' Hours Regulations

Key regulations include daily driving limits of 9 hours (extendable to 10 hours twice weekly), weekly limits of 56 hours, and mandatory break periods. Compliance is essential to avoid penalties and maintain safety standards.

Digital Tachograph Integration

Digital tachographs provide accurate, tamper-proof recording of driving time, speed, and distance. Integration with mobile calculators streamlines compliance monitoring and reporting.
