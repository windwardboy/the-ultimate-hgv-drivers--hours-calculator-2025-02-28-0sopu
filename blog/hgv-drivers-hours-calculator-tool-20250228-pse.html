HGV Drivers Hours Calculator Tool

February 28, 2024 | 15 minute read

In the world of logistics and transportation, managing HGV drivers' hours is a critical aspect of ensuring safety, compliance, and efficiency. The introduction of digital tachographs and advanced tools like the HGV Drivers Hours Calculator has revolutionized how fleet managers and drivers monitor and calculate drivers' hours.

Understanding HGV Drivers' Hours Regulations

Key regulations include:

  • Daily Driving Limit: Maximum 9 hours per day (extendable to 10 hours twice weekly)
  • Weekly Driving Limit: Maximum 56 hours
  • Breaks and Rest Periods: 45-minute break after 4.5 hours driving

The Role of the Digital Tachograph

The digital tachograph records:

  • Automatic Data Recording
  • Tamper-Proof Design
  • Data Storage for 28 days

Benefits of Using an HGV Drivers Hours Calculator

  • Enhanced Compliance

  • Better Decision-Making
  • Increased Driver Satisfaction

Steps to Use an HGV Drivers Hours Calculator

  • Install the Digital Tachograph
  • Insert the Digi Card
  • Connect Calculator to Tachograph
  • Input Additional Data
  • Monitor and Analyze

Choosing the Right Calculator
