Intuitive HGV Drivers Hours Calculator

February 28, 2024 | 12 minute read

In the world of logistics and transportation, managing Heavy Goods Vehicle (HGV) drivers' hours is a critical aspect of ensuring safety, compliance, and operational efficiency. With stringent regulations governing driving times, rest periods, and breaks, fleet managers and drivers alike need reliable tools to calculate drivers' hours accurately.

Understanding the Importance of HGV Drivers Hours Compliance

The rules governing HGV drivers' hours are designed to prevent fatigue-related accidents, ensuring the safety of drivers and other road users. These regulations are enforced by authorities across the globe, with strict penalties for non-compliance.

  • Maximum Driving Time: 9 hours per day (extendable to 10 hours twice weekly)
  • Daily Rest Periods: Minimum 11 hours
  • Weekly Driving Limits: Maximum 56 hours
  • Required Breaks: 45 minutes after 4.5 hours driving

The Role of Digital Tachographs

Digital tachographs are essential devices installed in vehicles to record driving time, speed, and rest periods. Working with a driver's digi card, they provide accurate, real-time data for monitoring compliance.
