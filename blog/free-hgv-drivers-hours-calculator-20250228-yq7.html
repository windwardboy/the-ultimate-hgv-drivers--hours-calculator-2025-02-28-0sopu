Managing HGV drivers' hours is a critical aspect of fleet operations, ensuring compliance with legal regulations while maintaining productivity and safety. With the advent of digital tachographs and advanced tools like the Free HGV Drivers Hours Calculator, fleet managers and drivers can now streamline their operations more effectively.

Understanding HGV Drivers' Hours Regulations

Key regulations include:

  • Daily driving limit: Maximum 9 hours (extendable to 10 hours twice weekly)
  • Weekly driving limit: Maximum 56 hours
  • Required breaks: 45 minutes after 4.5 hours of driving
  • Daily rest: Minimum 11 hours

The Role of Digital Tachographs

Digital tachographs record essential data including:

  • Driving time and duration
  • Rest periods
  • Vehicle speed
  • Distance covered
