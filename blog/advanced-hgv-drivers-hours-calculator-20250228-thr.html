Advanced HGV Drivers Hours Calculator

February 28, 2024 | 15 minute read

In the world of logistics and transportation, managing Heavy Goods Vehicle (HGV) drivers' hours is a critical aspect of ensuring compliance with legal regulations, maintaining safety standards, and optimizing operational efficiency.

Understanding HGV Drivers' Hours Regulations

Key regulations include:

  • Daily Driving Limit: Maximum 9 hours per day, extendable to 10 hours twice a week
  • Weekly Driving Limit: Maximum 56 hours per week
  • Fortnightly Driving Limit: Maximum 90 hours over two consecutive weeks
  • Mandatory breaks after 4.5 hours of driving

The Role of the Digital Tachograph

The digital tachograph is essential for:

  • Driver Identification via digi card
  • Automatic recording of driving times
  • Data storage for at least 28 days
  • Real-time compliance monitoring
