February 28, 2024 | 15 minute read

In the logistics and transportation industry, managing HGV drivers' hours is crucial for compliance, safety, and operational efficiency. Modern tools like the HGV Drivers Hours Calculator have revolutionized how we track and manage these essential metrics.

Understanding HGV Drivers' Hours Regulations

Key regulations that must be followed include:

  • Daily driving limit: Maximum 9 hours (extendable to 10 hours twice weekly)
  • Weekly driving limit: Maximum 56 hours
  • Required breaks: 45 minutes after 4.5 hours of driving
  • Minimum rest between shifts: 11 hours
