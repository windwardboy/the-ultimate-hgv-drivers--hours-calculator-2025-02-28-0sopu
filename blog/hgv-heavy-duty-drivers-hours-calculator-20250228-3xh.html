HGV Heavy Duty Drivers Hours Calculator

February 28, 2024 | 15 minute read

In the world of logistics and transportation, Heavy Goods Vehicle (HGV) drivers play a pivotal role in ensuring the smooth movement of goods across regions. Managing HGV drivers' hours is critical for compliance and safety.

Understanding HGV Drivers' Hours Regulations

In the UK and across the European Union, HGV drivers must follow strict regulations regarding driving hours and rest periods. These rules help prevent driver fatigue and ensure road safety.

Key Regulations

  • Maximum daily driving time: 9 hours (extendable to 10 hours twice weekly)
  • Daily rest period: Minimum 11 hours (can be split into 3+9 hours)
  • Weekly driving limit: 56 hours
  • Fortnightly limit: 90 hours over two consecutive weeks

The Role of Digital Tachographs

Digital tachographs are essential tools that record:

  • Driving time
  • Rest periods
  • Other work activities
  • Speed and distance

Using an HGV Hours Calculator

Modern HGV hours calculators offer several key features:

  • Automated calculations of driving hours
  • Real-time monitoring capabilities
  • Compliance alerts and warnings
  • Detailed reporting functions

Best Practices for Hours Management

  • Regular monitoring of driving hours
  • Proper maintenance of digital tachograph records
  • Prompt response to compliance alerts
  • Regular driver training on hours regulations
