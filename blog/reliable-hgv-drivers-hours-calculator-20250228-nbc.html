Reliable HGV Drivers Hours Calculator

February 28, 2024 | 12 minute read

In the logistics and transportation industry, managing HGV drivers' hours is crucial for compliance, safety, and efficiency. Modern tools like digital tachographs and HGV drivers hours calculators have become essential for fleet management.

Understanding HGV Drivers' Hours Regulations

Key regulations include daily driving limits of 9 hours (extendable to 10 hours twice weekly), weekly limits of 56 hours, and mandatory breaks after 4.5 hours of driving. Non-compliance can result in significant penalties.

The Role of Digital Tachographs

  • Automatic recording of driving time, speed, and distance
  • Integration with driver digi cards
  • Real-time monitoring capabilities
  • Secure data storage for up to 28 days
