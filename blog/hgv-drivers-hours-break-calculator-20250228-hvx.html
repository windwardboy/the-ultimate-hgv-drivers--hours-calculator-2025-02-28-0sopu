HGV Drivers Hours Break Calculator

February 28, 2024 | 12 minute read

Managing HGV drivers' hours is a critical aspect of ensuring road safety, compliance with regulations, and the well-being of drivers. The introduction of the digital tachograph has revolutionized how fleet managers and drivers monitor and manage their working hours.

Understanding HGV Drivers' Hours Regulations

The key regulations include:

  • Daily driving limit: Maximum 9 hours (extendable to 10 hours twice weekly)
  • Weekly driving limit: Maximum 56 hours
  • Fortnightly driving limit: Maximum 90 hours
  • Required breaks: 45 minutes after 4.5 hours driving

The Role of Digital Tachograph

The digital tachograph automatically records:

  • Driving time
  • Breaks and rest periods
  • Other work activities
  • Periods of availability
