UK HGV Drivers Hours Calculator

February 28, 2024 | 15 minute read

The transportation and logistics industry in the UK relies heavily on Heavy Goods Vehicle (HGV) drivers to keep goods moving efficiently across the country. Understanding and properly calculating drivers' hours is crucial for compliance and safety.

Understanding HGV Drivers' Hours Regulations

HGV drivers must comply with strict regulations regarding their working hours. Key limits include:

  • Daily driving limit: 9 hours (extendable to 10 hours twice weekly)
  • Weekly driving limit: 56 hours maximum
  • Fortnightly driving limit: 90 hours maximum
  • Required breaks: 45 minutes after 4.5 hours driving

The Role of Digital Tachographs

Digital tachographs are mandatory devices that record:

  • Driving time and speed
  • Rest periods and breaks
  • Distance traveled
  • Driver activities

Benefits of Using a Drivers Hours Calculator

  • Ensures accurate calculations
  • Saves time for fleet managers
  • Helps maintain compliance
  • Prevents driver fatigue

Essential Calculator Features

  • Customizable alerts
  • Comprehensive reporting

Future of Hours Management

  • AI and machine learning integration
  • Advanced telematics systems
